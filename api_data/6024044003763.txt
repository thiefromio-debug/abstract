공개
요약



본 발명은 슬래그의 부착량이 적고 안정된 용입 깊이를 가지며, 용접 이음새 강도 및 용접부의 내식성이 우수한 아크 용접 조인트 및 그 제조 방법을 제공하는 것을 목적으로 한다. 2장의 강판으로 이루어진 모서리를 접합하는 겹치기 필렛 아크 용접에 의해 제조되는 아크 용접 조인트로서, 상기 아크 용접 조인트는 상판, 하판 및 상기 상판과 상기 하판을 접합하는 용접부를 가지고 있으며 상판과 하판은 소정의 판 두께이며 상기 하판의 판 두께 t2와 용접부 단면에서의 상판과 하판의 경계에서 용접 비드의 하면까지의 거리인 용입 깊이 d가 소정의 식을 만족하고 용접 비드 표면의 비드 표면적 SBEAD와 슬래그로 덮인 영역의 면적인 슬래그 표면적SSLAG를 이용하여 소정의 식에서 산출되는 슬래그 피복 면적율 SRATIO가15% 이하인 아크 용접 조인트.





The objective of the present invention is to provide: an arc-welded joint with which a slag adhesion amount is small, which has a stable weld penetration depth, and which has excellent weld joint strength and welded portion corrosion resistance; and a method for manufacturing the arc-welded joint. This arc welded joint is manufactured by lap fillet arc welding in which a corner formed by two steel plates is joined, wherein: the arc welded joint has an upper plate, a lower plate, and a welded portion joining the upper plate and the lower plate; the upper plate and the lower plate each have a predetermined plate thickness; a plate thickness t 2 of the lower plate and a weld penetration depth d, which is the distance from a boundary between the upper plate and the lower plate and a lower surface of a welding bead in a cross-section through the welded portion, satisfies a predetermined formula; and a slag covering surface area ratio SRATIO is at most equal to 15%, the slag covering surface area ratio being calculated by a predetermined formula using a bead surface area SBEAD of the weld bead surface and a slag surface area SSLAG, which is the surface area of a region covered by slag.

An arc welded joint manufactured by overlapping fillet arc welding that joins corners made of two steel plates,
The arc welded joint has an upper plate, a lower plate, and a weld that joins the upper plate and the lower plate and is fused with the upper plate and the lower plate,
The thickness t1 of the upper plate and the thickness of the lower plate t2 are 5.0 mm or less, respectively,
The plate thickness t2 of the lower plate and the penetration depth d which is the distance from the boundary between the upper plate and the lower plate to the lower surface of the weld bead in the cross-section of the weld portion satisfy the following equation (1),
