공개
요약
The invention discloses a low-cost production method for producing an SGCC steel plate, which is based on C-Mn, adopts a low-carbon and low-silicon process technology, and adopts a controlled rolling and controlled cooling process principle of high initial rolling temperature and low curling temperature in hot rolling; and the rolling reduction rate of cold rolling is controlled to be 65%-87%, so that the forming performance of the hot-dip galvanized substrate is guaranteed. The produced SGCC steel plate is low in cost and excellent in performance.



本发明公开了一种低成本生产SGCC钢板的生产方法，以C‑Mn为基础，采取低碳低硅的工艺技术，热轧轧制采用高开轧温度、低温卷曲温度的控轧控冷工艺原则；冷轧轧制压下率控制在65％～87％，以便保证热镀锌基板成形性能。本发明所生产的SGCC钢板不仅成本低且性能优异。

대표청구항

청구항 0001항 (대표청구항)


1. A production method for producing SGCC steel plates at low cost is characterized by comprising the following steps: comprising the following steps:
(1) Heating the slab with the thickness of 67-72 mm to 1050-1100 ℃ and preserving heat for 30-50 minutes;
(2) The hot rolling is controlled to have a start rolling temperature of more than 1020 ℃ and a finish rolling temperature of 860-890 ℃; the heating temperature and the initial rolling temperature are 1030-1060 ℃,
(3) Cooling by adopting a partition group laminar cooling mode after hot rolling, and controlling the cooling speed and strength; the coiling temperature is 550-600 ℃;
(4) The rolling reduction rate of cold rolling is controlled to be 60% -87%;
(5) After cold rolling, the steel plate is subjected to laser welding by a welding machine, is subjected to electrolytic degreasing and cleaning, and enters a non-oxidation heating furnace for annealing heat treatment;
(6) The annealing temperature is 700-850 ℃; reasonably controlling the heat preservation time and the cooling speed;
(7) The annealed steel plate enters a zinc pot;
(8) Cooling the steel plate from the zinc pot by a cooling tower, and then feeding the steel plate into a leveling and straightening machine;
(9) The steel plate coming out of the straightener is subjected to surface passivation treatment and then packed into a finished product roll;
The SGCC steel plate comprises the following chemical components in percentage by weight: c:0.04 to 0.07 percent, si:0.015 to 0.050 percent, mn: 0.12-0.20%, P is less than or equal to 0.020%, S is less than or equal to 0.015%, alt is more than or equal to 0.015%, and the balance is Fe and unavoidable impurities.


1.一种低成本生产SGCC钢板的生产方法，其特征在于：包括：
(1)将厚度为67～72mm、板坯加热到1050～1100℃，保温30-50分钟；
(2)热轧轧制控制开轧温度大于1020℃，终轧温度为860～890℃；加热温度，开轧温度为1030～1060℃，
(3)热轧后采用隔组分散层流冷却模式进行冷却，控制冷却速度和强度；卷取温度在550～600℃；
(4)冷轧轧制压下率控制在60％～87％；
(5)冷轧后钢板经焊机激光焊接后，经电解脱脂清洗干净后进入无氧化加热炉进行退火热处理；
(6)退火温度700～850℃；合理控制保温时间及冷却速度；
(7)退火后的钢板进入锌锅；
(8)钢板从锌锅出来后经冷却塔冷却后进入平整、矫直机；
(9)从矫直机出来的钢板进行表面钝化处理后打包成成品卷；
所述SGCC钢板的重量百分比的化学组分为：C：0.04～0.07％，Si：0.015～0.050％，Mn：0.12～0.20％，P≤0.020％，S≤0.015％，Alt≥0.015％，其余为Fe及不可避免的夹杂。
