공개
요약



필렛 용접 장치 (100)는 스크린 (1)과 판재 (2)를 필렛 용접한다. 스크린 (1)은 소정의 간격 (g)으로 병렬로 배치된 복수의 장척 부재 (10)를 갖는다. 판재 (2)는 스크린 (1)에 재치된다. 필렛 용접 장치 (100)는 용접 토치 (4)와 모서리부 검출기 (5)와 용접 토치 이동 기구 (6)와 용접 전원 (7)을 구비한다. 용접 토치 (4)는 필렛 용접되는 부분에 용접 시공으로서 입열한다. 모서리부 검출기 (5)는 판재 (2)에서 필렛 용접되는 부분 중 스크린 (1)에서 먼 쪽의 모서리부 (25)를 검출한다. 용접 토치 이동 기구 (6)는 검출된 모서리부 (25)에 용접 토치 (4)를 향한다. 용접 전원 (7)은 모서리부 (25)로 향한 용접 토치 (4)에 펄스의 용접 전류 (C)를 흐르게 한다.





A fillet welding device (100) fillet-welds a screen (1) and a plate material (2). The screen (1) has a plurality of long members (10) arranged in parallel at a predetermined interval (g). The plate material (2) is placed on the screen (1). The fillet welding device (100) comprises: a welding torch (4); a corner part detector (5); a welding torch moving mechanism (6); and a welding power source (7). The welding torch (4) performs heat input as a welding procedure to portions that are to be fillet-welded. The corner part detector (5) detects a corner part (25) which is on the side far from the screen (1), among the portions that are to be fillet-welded in the plate material (2). The welding torch moving mechanism (6) directs the welding torch (4) toward the corner part (25) that is detected. The welding power source (7) applies a welding pulse current (C) to the welding torch (4) that is directed toward the corner part (25).

A fillet welding apparatus for welding fillet meat between a screen having a plurality of long members arranged in parallel at predetermined intervals and a plate material placed on the screen,
A welding torch that inputs heat to the portion to be welded as a welding work;
A corner detector for detecting a corner portion on the side far from the screen among the portion to be welded to the fillet in the plate material;
A welding torch moving mechanism for directing the welding torch to the detected corner portion;
A welding power source that passes a pulsed welding current through the welding torch directed at the corner, and
Fillet welding equipment.
