공개
요약
The invention relates to a composite solder for welding tin brass and a tungsten-titanium alloy and application of the composite solder to solve the problem that in the prior art, the color difference between a welding seam and a base material is large after welding. The composite solder comprises a central solder and an external solder arranged around the central solder; and the central welding flux is used for performing zinc-tin welding flux pool treatment on a welding material. The application comprises the step that the tin brass and the tungsten-titanium alloy are subjected to laser welding through the composite welding flux. According to the composite solder provided by the invention, the composite content is specifically designed, so that when the composite solder is used as the welding solder of the C46400 copper alloy and the tungsten-titanium alloy, the color difference between a welding seam obtained by welding and a base material C46400 copper alloy does not exist, and meanwhile, a welding joint has good performance.



本发明涉及一种用于锡黄铜和钨钛合金焊接的复合焊料及其用途，以解决现有技术中焊接后焊缝和基材色差较大的问题。所述复合焊料包括中心焊料和围绕所述中心焊料设置的外置焊料；所述中心焊料为将焊材进行锌锡焊料池处理。所述用途包括采用所述复合焊料将锡黄铜和钨钛合金进行激光焊接。本发明提供的复合焊料，通过对复合含量进行特定的设计，使得其作为C46400铜合金和钨钛合金的焊接焊料时，焊接所得焊缝与基体材料C46400铜合金的颜色无色差，同时焊接接头具有良好的性能。
